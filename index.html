Eating Georgian Food - Step By Step Guide

Acharuli Khachapuri - Georgian Cheeseboat

Do you like cheese, eggs and crunchy bread? If yes, Acharuli Khachapuri is made for you... Hold on, before you dig in let me give you some crucial tips... First, poach the egg yolk on top and delicately stir, untill the cheese becomes silky. It should not take more than 15 seconds... tear off that crunchy bread, dip it and enjoy!

Khinkali - Georgian Dumplin

There are so many things that could go wrong when eating Khinkali the very first time... I'm here to help you! To start, let's put away forks and knives, we don't need them. You only need your hands. Wait! Before you eat, Khinkali needs a final touch: black pepper; After you black peppered the Khinkali, grab it with your hands and take the first bite... drink all the juice inside and enjoy the rest!
